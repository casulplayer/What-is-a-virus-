What is a Computer Virus?

A computer virus is a type of malicious program that spreads by attaching itself to files or software. It can damage files, slow down your system, steal data, or spy on activity.

⚠️ Real viruses are dangerous — this site only simulates behavior for learning.

How Viruses Spread

  • Downloading unsafe files
  • Clicking suspicious links
  • Fake software installers
  • Email attachments from unknown senders

🛡️ How to Stay Safe

  • Keep your software updated
  • Don't download unknown files
  • Use antivirus protection
  • Enable firewall protection
  • Be careful with email links
