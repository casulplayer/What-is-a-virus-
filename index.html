How Computer Viruses Work

Learn how malware spreads, what damage it can cause, and how to stay safe online. This page is for cybersecurity awareness and education only.

What is a Virus?

A computer virus is malicious software that spreads by attaching itself to files or programs. It can damage data, slow down systems, or steal information.

How Viruses Spread

  • Fake downloads or cracked software
  • Phishing emails & attachments
  • Malicious websites
  • USB or external devices

What They Can Do

  • Corrupt or delete files
  • Steal personal data
  • Slow down your device
  • Allow unauthorized access

How to Stay Safe

  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Don’t download unknown files
  • Keep software updated
  • Use antivirus protection
