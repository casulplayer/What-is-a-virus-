How Computer Viruses Work

Learn how malware spreads, what damage it causes, and how to stay safe online. This is a cybersecurity awareness page for education only.

What is a Virus?

A computer virus is malicious software that spreads by attaching itself to files or programs. It can damage systems or steal information.

How Viruses Spread

  • Fake downloads
  • Phishing emails
  • Malicious websites
  • Cracked software

What They Do

  • Steal personal data
  • Corrupt files
  • Slow down devices
  • Give attackers access

How to Stay Safe

  • Avoid unknown links
  • Don’t download suspicious files
  • Keep software updated
  • Use antivirus protection

🛡️ Antivirus Protection

Antivirus software helps detect, block, and remove malware before it can harm your device.

  • Avira — Lightweight protection with real-time scanning
  • Malwarebytes — Strong malware & ransomware detection

Using antivirus software together with safe browsing habits gives the strongest protection against threats.
