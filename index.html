How Computer Viruses Work

Learn how viruses spread, what damage they can do, and how to protect your device safely. This page includes a simple simulation to visualize infection spreading.

What is a Virus?

A computer virus is a type of malicious software (malware) that spreads by copying itself into other files or systems. It can damage files, slow down systems, or steal data.

How Viruses Spread

  • Downloading infected files
  • Opening fake email attachments
  • Cracked software or cheats
  • Malicious websites

What Damage They Cause

  • Deleting or corrupting files
  • Stealing personal data
  • Slowing down your PC
  • Giving attackers remote access

How to Stay Safe

  • Don’t download unknown files
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Keep software updated
  • Use antivirus protection
